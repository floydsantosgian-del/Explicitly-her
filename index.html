Who We Are

Empowering the Next Generation of Women Leaders

Explicitly Her is a movement dedicated to shining a spotlight on the extraordinary women shaping the future of technology. Through personal stories, advocacy, and achievements, we celebrate the impact of women in our organization and beyond. We believe that by sharing stories of resilience and innovation, we inspire the next wave of change-makers.

Three defining chapters that tell the story of women who refused to be invisible who coded, led, advocated, and transformed our world.

Chapter 01
How It All Began

The Spark of Innovation

Every revolution starts with a single voice. Ours began when a group of determined women in our organization decided that silence was no longer an option. Armed with code, creativity, and conviction. They started building not just software, but a legacy.

Their work wasn't just technical, it was transformational, reshaping how we think about leadership and innovation in the tech world.

- Monica Jane S. Robiso

Polytechnic University of the Philippines

Chapter 02
Resilience in the Face of Challenges

Breaking Barriers

The path was never easy. They faced rooms where no one looked like them, meetings where their ideas were dismissed, and systems not designed with them in mind. But they persisted.

Each barrier they broke became a door for someone else. Their advocacy changed policies, their mentorship changed careers, and their presence changed the entire culture of our community.

- Summer Ann S. San Gabriel

Central Luzon State University

Chapter 03
A Legacy of Empowerment

Shaping the Future

Today, their impact inspires every goal I set, every skill I build, and every story I hope to tell. As a student, I may just be starting out, but women are already shaping the future I'm preparing to be part of.

From leading in classrooms to driving change in their communities, their contributions show what's possible. And this is just the beginning.

- Irish May Naag Arabaca

Polytechnic University of the Philippines
